Basic Facts

-A bicycle is a two-wheeled, human-powered or motor-assisted vehicle.

-Introduced in 19th-century Europe, bicycles outnumber cars by the 21st century, with over 1 billion in use.

-Used for transport, recreation, fitness, and professional purposes.

- Basic design unchanged since 1885, with improvements from modern materials and technology. -Electric bicycles have gained popularity.

- Bicycle invention influenced the development of key automobile components like ball bearings, pneumatic tires, and chain-driven sprockets.

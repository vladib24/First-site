Ethymology

- The word "bicycle" first appeared in English in 1868, describing two-wheeled vehicles in Paris.

- It was used earlier in 1847 in French for an unidentified two-wheeled carriage.

- Synonyms for "bicycle" include "bike," "pushbike," "pedal cycle," and "cycle."

- In India, "cycle" refers to pedal-powered vehicles, while "bike" refers to motorized ones.

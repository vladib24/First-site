Uses

- Bicycles are used for transportation, commuting, and utility cycling.

- They are used professionally by mail carriers, paramedics, police, messengers, and delivery services.

- Military uses include communications, reconnaissance, troop movement, supply, and patrol, such as in bicycle infantry.

- Recreational uses include bicycle touring, mountain biking, fitness, and play.

- Bicycle sports include racing, BMX, track racing, criteriums, roller racing, sportives, and time trials.
